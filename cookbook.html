Beef Stew

Ingredients
6 servings
2 lbs stew beef
1/3 tsp garlic salt, pepper
1/4 cup flour
3 TBSP olive oil
2 TBSP butter
1 onion, diced
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 TBSP red wine vinegar
4 cups beef broth
2 tsp beef bouillon
1 1/2 TBSP worcestershire
2 TBSP tomato paste
4 carrots, sliced
1 lb yukon gold potatoes
2 bay leaves
2 tsp rosemary
1 cup frozen peas

- Chop: beef (if not already cut), onion, carrots, and potatoes. Set aside.

- Sear meat: heat olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Sprinkle beef with garlic salt + pepper. Toss to combine. Coat with flour. Once oil is hot, sear each piece of meat for 45 seconds front & back. Transfer to crockpot.

- Sauté veggies: to now-empty skillet, melt butter & sauté onions + garlic for about 5 min. Transfer to crockpot.

- Add ingredients: to the crockpot, add everything but the frozen peas (red wine vinegar + beef broth + bouillon + worcestershire + tomato paste + carrots + potatoes + bay leaves + rosemary).

- Cook on LOW for 7-8 hours or HIGH for 3-4 hours.

- To thicken: can make a cornstarch slurry (3 TBSP cornstarch + 3 TBSP water) and add in the last 15 minutes of cooking. Optional but I recommend it.

- Add peas: also in the last 15 minutes of cooking. Stir.

- Serve: and enjoy
